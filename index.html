Welcome

About Penuel Stopover

Strategically located on the route to Kimana Wildlife Reserve, Penuel Stopover is your ultimate gateway facility. We understand the needs of safari-bound travelers and have designed every service around your convenience.

From authentic local cuisine to professional vehicle maintenance, our mission is to ensure you arrive at the reserve relaxed, refueled, and ready for adventure. We pride ourselves on quality service, competitive pricing, and the warm Kenyan hospitality that makes every visit memorable.
